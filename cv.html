INTERNATIONAL ARBITRATION PRACTICES⋮

Mr. Noshadha has market-leading experience of handling the most sophisticated arbitration cases in various capacities including serving as party nominated arbitrator in ad hoc and administered arbitration proceedings, sole arbitrator, the chairman of the arbitral tribunal, and a legal advisor to a party. Mr. Noshadha is a chartered transcontinental arbitrator, arbitrator and permanent representative of the American International Commercial Arbitration, arbitrator at the London Court of International Arbitration, Arbitrator at the BANI Arbitration Centre (Indonesia), Arbitrator for the Vakeel International Arbitration and Mediation Center (India) and also experienced arbitrator and representative of parties in different International Commercial, Investment and Sport Arbitrations which gives Mr. Noshadha an unprecedented insight into the proceedings of arbitration institutions of foreign jurisdictions. Mr. Noshadha extensive experience in international trade, investment, and sport laws includes developing, negotiating, insight and handling complex transactions of contracts which they are directly or indirectly related to commercial, investment, and sport cases.
Mr. Noshadha experienced at the CAS, LCIA, ICSID, ICC, SCC, AAA, UNCITRAL, VIAC, SIAC, WTO, CIETAC, BANI, UICA rules, and Swiss Rules of International Arbitration. He is an arbitrator with professional practices in agriculture, investment, energy and resources, sport, oil and gas, pharmaceutical, and metallurgy industries.

  • 2015 - Present, Arbitrator and official representative of the American International Commercial Arbitration Court (USA);
  • 2016 – 2019, Arbitrator at the Ukrainian International Commercial Arbitration Court (Ukraine).
