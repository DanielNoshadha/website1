One of the areas of Noshadha International Law Firm practices is Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR) methods to regulate disputes before applying international commercial, investment, sport and maritime arbitration courts.
ADR methods are more quick, less price, with high information privacy and friendly dispute resolution ways.
Noshadha International Law Firm ADR lawyers may assist you for international commercial, investment, sport and maritime dispute resolutions via following ADR methods:
  • Mediation;
  • Conciliation;
  • Negotiation;
  • Bon Offeces;
  • Enquete;
  • Expert determination.
